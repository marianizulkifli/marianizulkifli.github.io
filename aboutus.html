ABOUT US

Nurianiz Fashion provides a collection of clothes
Muslimah especially for women who want to be stylish. Among the muslimah clothes that we provide are dress, baju kurung and blouse while for hijab there are various types to choose from, namely shawl, bawal, and khimar. Apart from shirts and hijabs, we also provide other Muslimah accessories such as handsocks and so on. 

Vision

Nurianiz Fashion has a vision to become one of the famous product around the world that helps muslim woman to cover their aurah well in addition to being stylish woman.

Mission

Nurianiz Fashion's mission is to produce, and sell quality clothing products, for example fabrics of various style, comfortable, thick and long as well as a variety of colors to suit the tastes of buyers. In addition, Nurianiz Fashion has a mission to sell affordable merchandise for all walks of life, especially teenagers.
